Before you begin

You are about to work through a fifteen-step scenario set in Klaipėda and the wider Baltic region. At each step you will see two things side by side: what is actually happening, and what is circulating on your phone. You will be asked to make a decision. There are no correct answers displayed in advance. Make the choice you would actually make — not the one you think you should make. The debrief comes at the end.
